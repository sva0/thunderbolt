Transform your NAS into a Thunderbolt™ 3 NAS

By installing a Thunderbolt™ 3 PCIe card into your QNAP NAS, it is instantly transformed into a Thunderbolt™ 3 NAS! Multiple Thunderbolt™-equipped workstations can be connected to the NAS to achieve greater teamwork efficiency in 4K workflows, file storage, backup, and sharing.

Upgrade your NAS with a Thunderbolt™ 3 PCIe card

By installing a Thunderbolt™ 3 PCIe card into your QNAP NAS, it is instantly transformed into a Thunderbolt™ 3 NAS! Multiple Thunderbolt™-equipped workstations can be connected to the NAS to achieve greater teamwork efficiency in 4K workflows, file storage, backup, and sharing.
